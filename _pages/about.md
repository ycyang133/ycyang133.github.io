I received the bachelor’s and master’s degrees in 2018 and 2021, respectively, from Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU), Xi’an, China, and the Ph.D. degree in information and communication engineering from the Center of Intelligent Acoustics and Immersive Communications (CIAIC), NPU, Xi’an, China, in 2025. I was a Visiting Ph.D. student with Waseda University, Kitakyushu, Japan, from November 2023 to November 2025. In 2026, I joined Tencent as a researcher. My research interests include array signal processing, speech enhancement, blind source separation, and machine learning. I am an active reviewer of IEEE Signal Processing Letters, EURASIP Journal on Audio, Speech, and Music Processing, Journal of Computational and Applied Mathematics, and APSIPA ASC.
